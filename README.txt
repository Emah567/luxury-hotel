# UTree Hotel - Luxury Hotel Website

!https://utree-hotel.vercel.app/

## Overview

UTree Hotel is a sophisticated and elegant luxury hotel website that offers an exceptional user experience. This project is dedicated to providing a responsive design and captivating animations on scroll, ensuring that your visitors are immersed in a world of luxury and comfort.

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: A mobile-first design approach ensures that the website looks stunning on all devices, including desktop, tablet, and mobile.

- **Modern and Elegant**: A sleek and modern user interface that reflects the luxury and sophistication of UTree Hotel.

- **Animate on Scroll**: Engage your visitors with elegant animations triggered on scroll, providing an interactive and immersive experience.

- **Easy Navigation**: A user-friendly interface with intuitive navigation, making it effortless for guests to explore and book their stay.

- **Luxurious Imagery**: High-quality images showcasing the hotel's amenities and stunning surroundings.
